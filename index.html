My Journey thus far

Aaron Nkansah Boateng is a young ambitious individual with a strong passion for data analytics. Holding a Bsc. Chemistry from the prestigious Kwame Nkrumah Univeristy of Science and Technology, Aaron has consistently demostrated his thirst for analytics and commitment to making an impact in whatever team he finds himself.

Aaron is currently an Agency Supervisor with Prudential Life Insurance where he manages a team of Financial Consultants to meet and exceed company targets. His dilligence to work earned him the overall best rookie Team leader and Best Rookie Agency Supervisor in 2021 and 2022 respectively. A two time Africa Diamond Club Winner, Aaron also won the Prudential All Leaders Conference and was a finalist for the Presidents Club Award.

Aaron is currently pursuing a Data Analytics BootCamp with Alex the Analyst and has also qualified for a fully funded Data Analytics Course with ALX in partnership with Mastercard. As the world becomes increasingly data driven, he plans on leveraging data and analytics to help his employers make more informed and strategic decisions.
